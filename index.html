Club Overview

The North South University Social Services Club (NSUSSC) is one of the premier student organizations at NSU. Dedicated to the betterment of society, we organize large-scale charitable events, relief efforts, and cultural programs. Joining NSUSSC is not just about club activities; it's about building character, leadership, and making a tangible difference in the lives of the underprivileged.

Our Signature Events

A glimpse into the impact we create throughout the year.

Socio Camp

An inter-university idea generating competition to solve social problems.

Flood Relief

Providing emergency aid, food, and medicine to flood-affected areas across Bangladesh.

Winter Clothing Drive

Collecting and distributing warm clothes to the homeless during harsh winters.

Onushongo

A dedicated program for underprivileged children to enjoy a day of fun and learning.

Pitha Uthsob

Celebrating Bengali culture with traditional cakes and cultural performances.

Blood Donation Drive

Regular drives to collect blood for critical patients and raise awareness.

Amar Pathshala

An educational initiative providing free schooling materials to needy students.

Iftar Distribution & Eid Clothing

This event typically holds in an orphanage with the participation of club members.

Boishakhi Mela

A vibrant celebration of the Bengali New Year promoting our heritage.
