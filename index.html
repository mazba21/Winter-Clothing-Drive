The Mission

Every winter, thousands of families across Bangladesh face the harsh cold without adequate protection. At NSU Social Services Club, we believe that warmth is a basic right, not a privilege.


Our Winter Clothing Drive is one of our flagship events. For weeks, our dedicated members collected donations, organized logistics, and traveled to remote villages to ensure that help reached those who needed it most. This isn't just about clothes; it's about showing compassion and solidarity.
